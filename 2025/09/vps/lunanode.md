# lunanode

## sign up

1. https://lunanode.com/infrastructure
2. click SIGN UP
3. enter E-mail address
4. enter Password
5. select country
6. click Sign Up
7. verify your e-mail
8. click Billing
9. click Add Credit
10. enter Phone number
11. click Continue
12. enter Code
13. click Submit
14. click Add Credit
15. click Add Funds via Credit Card
16. enter Name
17. enter Address
18. enter ZIP
19. enter City
20. click Payment Info
21. enter Card number
22. enter expiration
23. enter CVC
24. click Pay

## create VM

1. click Create VM
2. click Canada
3. enter Hostname `hello`
4. click a Plan
5. click Debian
6. click Create virtual machine
